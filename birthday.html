Birthday fundraiser

Some people have been asking me what I want for my 30th. The trouble is there's basically nothing I need right now. So in lieu of a present, I'm asking for a donation on my behalf to whichever organisation you believe can do the most good with it.

My only request is that you take seriously the 'the most good' part. I.e. I'd like you to put some thought and research into not just what charities could do a lot of good with it, but which single charity you believe would do the most good out of any charity with it.

I'm interested to see what charities people ending up deciding on, so do email me to let me know.

If you're not sure where to get started, try Introduction to Effective Altruism and GiveWell. Email me if you have any questions.
